Typographical ornaments can be typest using the \texttt{WebOMints} fonts available at ctan
\url{http://tug.ctan.org/tex-archive/fonts/webomints/}. They were designed by the Galapagos Design Group \url{http://www.galapagosdesign.com/download/index.html}.
The border and ornament font is called
Web-O-Mints.  Created by George Ryan, Web-O-Mints contains a
rich assortment of typographic decorations inspired by
historical sources. The \texttt{Web-O-Mints} font is available in TrueType or PostScript
format, for use on PCs or Macs.  The collection includes all the files needed to use this font in \LaTeX and three small LaTeX examples.
